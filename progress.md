## How to mark a day "done"

A day is complete when you can tick **all four boxes**:

1. **Read** – You read the day's section in the README.
2. **Code** – You wrote (or typed out) the day's example code.
3. **Test** – You ran the related exercise and its tests pass (`python3 -m pytest ...`).
4. **Reflect** – You wrote a brief note in the Reflections column.
